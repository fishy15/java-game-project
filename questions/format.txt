Here is how the structure of the questions.txt. file works. 
If the line is inside angle brackets, you have to fill that part in
yourself. 

If the question uses Radio Buttons, then the format is below:

<question code>
<number of lines>
<question>
radio
<number of answer choices>
correct <number of correct answers>
<answer choice>
<reason why it is correct>
(continue for all correct answer choices)
alternate <number of alternate answers>
<answer choice>
<reason why it is alternate>
(continue for all alternate answers)
wrong <number of wrong answers>
<answer choice>
<reason why it is wrong>
(continue for all wrong answer choices)
